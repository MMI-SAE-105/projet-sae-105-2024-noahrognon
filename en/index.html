The Era of Prosthetics

A World Without Limits

Welcome to a future where technology and the human body merge in unprecedented ways. Through the exploration of prosthetics, this project delves into their evolution from the past, present, and future. How might prosthetics, once simple tools, one day surpass human capabilities? Discover the answers through these five articles.
