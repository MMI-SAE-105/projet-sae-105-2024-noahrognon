About Me

My name is Noah Rognon, a first-year student in the BUT MMI program in Montbéliard. This project, The Era of Prosthetics, was born from my interest and research in science and technology, particularly innovations that push human limits.

The Project: The Era of Prosthetics

Through this site, I aim to explore how prosthetics, once simple compensatory tools, are becoming augmented extensions of the human body.

University Context

This site was created as part of my academic curriculum, with the goal of providing informative and interactive content on a futuristic innovation topic. I chose to focus on prosthetics because they embody the perfect fusion of biotechnology and engineering and raise fascinating questions about the future of humanity.

I thank my teachers for their support and guidance throughout this project.
